How to Play Lô Tô - Viet Bingo

WELCOME to Lô Tô - Viet Bingo

Lô Tô is a common game during Tết (Lunar New Year) in Vietnam. It is a kind of gamble but just play it for fun on Tết or any occasion.

First of all, download the tickets and print out.

There are 300 tickets in this file. So the game can be played up to 300 people at the same time (1 ticket/person). Players need to purchage for the ticket. You can price a ticket (eg, $1, $5). One person can purchage more than one ticket, so get more chance to win.

Nominate a person to pick number by clicking the "pick a number" button. The numbers will be generated ramdomlly. The nominated person could play at the same time. Just for fun.

When a number is generated, if the players have the number on their ticket(s), mark the number. Keep playing until anyone gets five numbers in a row, they win. It could be more than one person win the game at the same time. However, you need to keep this in mind, when the players get four numbers in a row, they need to say out loud "chờ" (waiting). And then, when they get five numbers, they must yell "KINH" (win). If the players do not say those words, they can't win.

Let's play!
